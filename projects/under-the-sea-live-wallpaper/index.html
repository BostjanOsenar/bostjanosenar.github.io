Under the Sea Live Wallpaper

A live wallper for Android devices available on Google Play

A live wallpaper for Android devices that is ending its 4th year on Google Play with more than 1.7 million downloads and an average score of 4.0. The project took roughly six months to complete and was first published on Google Play in late 2012.

Made using OpenGL ES 2.0 in its purest form. Just shaders over very simple geometry. It was optimised for high-end devices availaibe at launch time, devices like Samsung Galaxy S2. Designed and developed in collaboration with Goran Golubovic and published under Aksis Plus.
